Kort

An OpenStreetMap Game

  • follow @KortGame

OpenStreetMap

Kort helps to improve OpenStreetMap-data. Tasks to be done are shown on a map.

Players can check already fulfilled tasks regarding their correctness. As soon as enough positive examinations have been made, the solution of a task may be inserted in OpenStreetMap.

Gamification

Kort uses Gamification to motivate users to play with the app. For both, solving tasks and checking existing solutions, points (so called Koins) can be earned. The goal is to continually rise through the ranks of the high score list. Additionally, the player is also awarded medals for his efforts.

WebApp

Kort is a WebApp, which is written in HTML5 and Javascript. Through this, multiple mobile operation systems are supported. These include iOS, Android and Blackberry.

The App is launched and played in the browser of the smartphone.

Supported browsers

Kort was developed for usage on mobile devises (Smartphones, tablets). However, due to the Sencha Touch 2 Frameworks, all WebKit-Browser are supported as well.
